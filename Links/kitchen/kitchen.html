Chicken Masala Recipe

Halal Chicken and Rice

Marinated in oregano, lemon, and coriander, chicken thighs are browned, chopped into chunks and served over turmeric-yellow rice. A salad of iceberg and tomatoes, and mysterious sweet-sour-tangy white sauce that just has to be ladled all over the chicken and rice for true street-style authenticity.

Cheese Cake Recipe

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert. Consists of a mixture of soft, fresh cheese, eggs, vanilla and sugar. Bottom layer often consists of a base made from crushed cookies (or digestive biscuits), graham crackers, pastry, or sponge cake.
